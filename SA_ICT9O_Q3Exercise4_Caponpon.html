Boracay

A little island in the center of the Philippines is called Boracay. It is renowned for its beaches and resorts. White Beach is surrounded by palm trees, bars, and eateries along the west shore. Strong winds on the east coast make Bulabog Beach a hotspot for water sports. The nearby Mount Luho observation platform provides sweeping views of the island. Diverse marine life inhabits shipwrecks and coral reefs offshore.

Me and my family visited Boracay during 2016. We all had fun because we tried all of the activities like the Banana boat, Parasailing, Scuba Diving, etc. That was when my family members flew back here in the Philippines from the U.S. So we were all complete that time.
